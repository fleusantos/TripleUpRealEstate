Triple Up Real Estate – Help Section

Welcome to the Help Section of Triple Up Real Estate! We’re here to guide you through every step of your real estate journey. Whether you’re buying, selling, or investing, we provide the resources and answers you need. Below are common areas where we can help.

1. Buying a Property
If you are a firt time buyer, renter, or home owner wanting to get out from under a heavy mortgage, we have the program for you. No finiance charges, just taxes, insurance, and principal payments only. This is NOT for acquiring rental property.

Property Search: You pick the property you want and we do the rest. We negotiate the deal, secure the property and provide you with an agreement to own the property with just principal payments only.

2. Selling a Property
Selling your home can be stressful, but we’re here to make it easier:
Property Valuation: Get an accurate market valuation of your home. Start with our valuation expert.
Marketing Your Property: We have more buyers than sellers so marketing may not even be necessay.
Negotiation & Offers: Our experienced agents will negotiate the best price and terms on your behalf.
Closing: We’ll take care of the final steps, ensuring a smooth transaction from offer to close. For more details, visit our Seller’s Guide.

3. Investing in Real Estate
Thinking about real estate as an investment? Our experts offer personalized advice and strategies for maximizing returns:
Market Analysis: We provide in-depth market analysis and trends to help you make informed decisions.
This is more of a loan than an investment with the returns equaling 9% annually.
4. Financing & Mortgage Assistance
We completely remove the need for financing or mortgages. Just pay taxes, insurance, and a min principal payment of 1/360th of the total sale price.
