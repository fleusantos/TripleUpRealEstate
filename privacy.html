Privacy Policy for Triple Up Real Estate

Effective Date: October 1st, 2024
Triple Up Real Estate ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of our clients and website visitors. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you visit our website, https://tripleuprealestate.com, or use our services.

1. Information We Collect
We may collect the following types of information:
• Personal Information: This includes your name, email address, phone number, and other details you provide when filling out forms or communicating with us.
• Non-Personal Information: This includes data collected automatically through your interaction with our website, such as browser type, IP address, device information, and browsing patterns (cookies, pixels).

2. How We Use Your Information
We may use the information collected for the following purposes:
• To Provide Services: Your personal information helps us to communicate, schedule appointments, and provide real estate services.
• Marketing and Communication: With your consent, we may send you promotional content and newsletters.
• Analytics: To improve our website's functionality, user experience, and service offerings by analyzing user behavior and preferences.

3. Sharing of Information
We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. However, we may share information with:
• Service Providers: Third-party companies or individuals that assist with website hosting, marketing, or operational services.
• Legal Compliance: If required by law, we may disclose your personal information to regulatory authorities.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies
We use cookies to enhance your experience on our website. Cookies are small text files stored on your browser that help us understand how you interact with our site. You can modify your browser settings to block or delete cookies, though doing so may affect your experience.

5. Data Security
We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data.

6. Your Privacy Rights
Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:
• Access, correct, or delete the personal information we have collected about you.
• Opt-out of marketing communications.
• Restrict or object to certain data processing activities.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@tripleuprealestate.com.

7. Third-Party Links
Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites. Please review their privacy policies separately.

8. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page regularly.
