What are Down Payment Credits?

Downpayment credits reduce the amount you pay upfront for a home. To purchase a home from Triple Up Real Estate you will need 10% of the value of the home in Down Payment Credits. Start building your Down Payment Credit balance now by playing free games, trying free sites, helping with 360WIN's GoFundMe campaign, and more. For example, a $100,000 home requires a $10,000 downpayment. Once you acquire $10,000 in downpayment credits, you can purchase this home with zero down. The cost to own a $100,000 home from Triple Up Real Estate would be less than $525 per month and this includes the taxes and insurance.
