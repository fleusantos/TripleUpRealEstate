Costs involved in selling your property typically include:

7. What should I know before investing in real estate?
Real estate investment can be highly profitable, but it usually involves risks. That is why we back all investments and loans with 32.5% more equity than the funding.
• Safe: Backed by 32.5% more equity than your investment
• Lucrative: Receive 9% annually plus there are often bonuses.
• Fast Turn Around: Instead of taking 30 years to pay off a home loan or investment, our turn around time is always less than 2 years and often just months.

One of the reasons we offer our investors 32.5% more equity than their investment is so we can allow sales for more than the appraised value. The extra cannot be way over value though, it must stay within reason.

No financing charges apply to property we sell. Buyers pay taxes, insurance, and principal payments only. Even the downpayment can be converted to principal payments only with several options to acquire Down Payment Credits.
• Qualifying is Easy
• No Credit Check
• No Income Verification Needed
• Just pay taxes, insurance, and principal payments.
The minimum principal payment is equal to 1/360th of the purchase price.
